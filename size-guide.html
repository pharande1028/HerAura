1 One Size - Universal Fit

What does "One Size" mean?

Many of our jewellery pieces are designed with a "One Size" fit, meaning they are crafted to comfortably fit most people without requiring specific sizing. This makes shopping easier and ensures you get the perfect piece every time!

One Size Items Include:

  • Adjustable Rings: Feature expandable bands or open designs that can be gently adjusted to fit fingers from size 5 to 9
  • Adjustable Bracelets: Come with extension chains (typically 2-3 inches) allowing you to customize the fit for wrists ranging from 6 to 8.5 inches
  • Standard Length Necklaces: Designed at optimal lengths (16-18 inches for chokers, 18-20 inches for standard) that suit most neck sizes
  • Adjustable Necklaces: Feature extender chains for customizable length
  • Standard Earrings: Studs and drop earrings that fit standard ear piercings
