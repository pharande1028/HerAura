Exchange Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

Our Promise: We want you to love your HerAura jewelry. If you're not completely satisfied, we offer hassle-free returns and exchanges within 15 days of delivery.

1. Return & Exchange Period

You have 15 days from the date of delivery to return or exchange your jewelry. Items must be returned in their original condition with all packaging materials.

2. Eligible Items

Items that CAN be returned/exchanged:

  • Unworn jewelry in original condition
  • Items with original packaging and tags
  • Products with manufacturing defects
  • Wrong items delivered

Items that CANNOT be returned/exchanged:

  • Customized or personalized jewelry
  • Items worn or damaged by customer
  • Products without original packaging
  • Items returned after 15 days

3. Return Process

Step 1: Contact our customer service team at heraura26@gmail.com or +91 9356948452 within 15 days of delivery.
Step 2: Provide your order number and reason for return. We'll send you a return authorization and shipping label.
Step 3: Pack the item securely in original packaging and attach the return label.
Step 4: Drop off the package at the designated pickup location or schedule a pickup.
Step 5: Once we receive and inspect the item, we'll process your refund or exchange within 3-5 business days.

4. Exchange Policy

We offer exchanges for:

  • Different size (if applicable)
  • Different design of equal or lesser value
  • Store credit for future purchases

If exchanging for a higher-value item, you'll need to pay the difference. If exchanging for a lower-value item, we'll issue store credit for the difference.

5. Refund Policy

Refund Methods:

  • Original Payment Method: Refunds will be processed to your original payment method
  • Processing Time: 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item
  • Refund Amount: Full purchase price minus any applicable return shipping costs

6. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

  • Contact us immediately with photos of the damage
  • We'll arrange free return shipping
  • Choose between full refund or replacement
  • Priority processing within 24-48 hours

7. Quality Guarantee

All HerAura jewelry comes with our anti-tarnish guarantee. If your jewelry tarnishes within 6 months of purchase under normal wear conditions, we'll replace it free of charge.

8. Store Credit

Store credits:

  • Valid for 12 months from issue date
  • Can be used for any HerAura products
  • Non-transferable and cannot be converted to cash
  • Can be combined with other offers

9. Special Circumstances

Festival/Sale Items:

Items purchased during special sales or festivals follow the same return policy but may have extended processing times during peak seasons.

Gift Returns:

Gift recipients can return items for store credit without the original receipt. Original purchaser information may be required for verification.
