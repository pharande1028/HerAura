Shipping Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

1. Shipping Areas

We currently ship to all locations within India. International shipping is not available at this time but will be introduced soon.

2. Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (Monday to Friday, excluding holidays). Orders placed on weekends will be processed on the next business day.

3. Shipping Methods & Delivery Times

Shipping Method Delivery Time Cost
Standard Shipping 5-7 business days ₹99 (Free on orders above ₹999)
Express Shipping 2-3 business days ₹199
Same Day Delivery (Select Cities) Within 24 hours ₹299

4. Free Shipping

Enjoy free standard shipping on all orders above ₹999. This offer is automatically applied at checkout.

5. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can track your package using:

  • Our website's order tracking page
  • Direct carrier tracking websites
  • WhatsApp updates on your registered number

6. Shipping Address

Please ensure your shipping address is complete and accurate. We are not responsible for packages delivered to incorrect addresses provided by the customer.

Address Requirements:

  • Full name of recipient
  • Complete address with landmark
  • Correct PIN code
  • Active phone number

7. Delivery Attempts

Our delivery partners will make up to 3 delivery attempts. If delivery is unsuccessful after 3 attempts, the package will be returned to us, and you may be charged for re-shipping.

8. Packaging

All jewelry is carefully packaged in our signature HerAura boxes with protective padding to ensure safe delivery. Each package includes:

  • Elegant HerAura jewelry box
  • Care instructions card
  • Authenticity certificate
  • Protective bubble wrap

9. Damaged or Lost Packages

If your package arrives damaged or is lost during transit, please contact us immediately at heraura26@gmail.com with your order number and photos (if applicable). We will arrange for a replacement or full refund.

10. Holidays and Delays

Shipping times may be extended during peak seasons, festivals, or due to unforeseen circumstances. We will notify customers of any expected delays.
